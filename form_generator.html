Team Evaluation

Grading Rules when inputting a value into the participation level box

  • absent = F
  • unsatisfactory = D- to C+
  • satisfactory = B+ to A
  • above and beyond = A+

Rules for inputting data

  • All team members names must be in alphabetical characters
  • Input team name, must use alphabetical characters
  • Overallgrade can be alphabetical characters (+/-) A,B,C,D,F
  • All time formats (start time and end time) must be in the form: hour:minute (i.e: 11:25)
